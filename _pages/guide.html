Quick Use Guide

Sign in securely

Logging into your Parish.es account is easy. You'll be able to immediately access your dashboard.

Tip: Each member of your parish management team can have a unique login, with separate permissions.

Easily create new pages

Start building your site immediately! Creating new pages is as easy as writing an email.

Go mobile, instantly

Your site is automatically mobile-ready! Our templates are designed with mobile-first usage in mind, including page layouts and navigation.

Tip: Close to 80% of most parish website users visit by phone.
