FAQs

Is there a setup fee?

No!

We understand that a lot of software companies charge setup, or onboarding, fees to get things started. At Parish.es we're primarily motivated to get our helpful tool into the hands of as many parishes as possible.
So we're offering free setup to our new customers (a $300 value).
We'll help move your page content, images, and contact information to your new Parish.es site for free. And then we'll make sure you're confident managing your content after launch.
We can’t promise this offer will last forever — so take advantage while you can!

Do you provide customer support?

Absolutely!

We're committed to working closely with our customers. Our team is friendly, and super fast to respond!

Can I use my own domain?

Yes!

We will set you up with a temporary domain on Parish.es, e.g. yourparishname.parish.es. However, you can also use a custom domain that you own for your primary URL.

How long does it take to set up my new website?

5 minutes

Setting up your Parish.es template is quick and easy. You can launch your new website in a matter of minutes.
Building out the template and adding all your individual content and personal touches takes as much time as you'd like to spend. Don’t forget, we're offering free setup assistance to new customers — so take advantage!

Can I customize my site?

Yes!

We've been building custom websites for years. And we know the importance of making a website yours.
Our main goal in building Parish.es was to create a simple and easy to use platform, and to ensure that the most relevant, needed information is always front and center. But we still give you control over key elements, like images, videos, and branding.

Do I still need web hosting?

No!

If you already have a website, you're probably paying for web hosting (e.g., through GoDaddy, Network Solutions, or another vendor). Parish.es is an all-in-one platform — so you can probably cancel your web hosting and save some money!

Will this affect my email?

No!

Parish.es is not an email server. If you have domain-based email like yourname@yourparish.com your email is controlled by MX records stored in your domain DNS. Those settings are different from the settings that control which website your domain name points to. So there is no reason you would have to change your email service because you use the Parish.es website platform.

Can I use forms / calendars?

Yes!

You can easily integrate third-party forms and calendars. There are lots of great options that work directly, like Google Calendar, Google Forms, Formspree, and others.
